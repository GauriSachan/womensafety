üõ°Ô∏è Core Self-Defense Techniques

1. Awareness & Prevention

Staying alert is the first step. Avoid distractions like phones, and trust your instincts if a place or person feels unsafe.

2. Escaping Holds

Learn quick ways to break free from wrist grabs, chokeholds, or unwanted touches using leverage and body mechanics.

3. Target Weak Spots

In emergencies, aim for sensitive areas: eyes, nose, throat, knees, or groin. Quick strikes here can create time to escape.

4. Everyday Objects

Your handbag, keys, umbrella, or even a pen can act as powerful tools for self-defense when used effectively.

5. Verbal Defense

A strong, commanding voice can startle an attacker and attract attention, reducing the chance of escalation.

6. Quick Escape

The goal is not to fight, but to survive. Learn to create opportunities to escape quickly and reach safety.
