🚨 Cyberbullying Emergency Help

If you or someone you know is being cyberbullied, take action immediately. You are not alone.

📞 Call Child Helpline (1098 - India)

Important Helplines

India

Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930

Women Helpline: 1091

Child Helpline: 1098

United States

National Suicide Prevention: 988

CyberTipline: Report Online

Global

UNICEF Help Portal: Visit

Interpol Cybercrime: Report

What To Do Immediately

  • 📌 Do not respond to bullies online.
  • 🚫 Block & Report the offender on social media.
  • 📷 Take Screenshots of abusive messages or posts.
  • 👮 File a complaint with the nearest cybercrime cell or police.
  • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Talk to someone you trust – family, teacher, or friend.
