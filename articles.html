Understanding Cyberbullying

Cyberbullying is one of the most pressing issues faced by women today. It includes online harassment, trolling, and the spread of harmful content that can deeply affect mental health and safety. Awareness, reporting, and learning self-defense strategies are essential to build resilience against online abuse. SafeHer is committed to providing resources, support, and a safe community for women everywhere.
