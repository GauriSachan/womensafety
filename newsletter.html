Cybersecurity Threats Against Women

Understanding the risks and empowering you with SafeHer

The Growing Dangers in Cyber Space

In today's digital age, women face increasing threats online — from cyberstalking, phishing scams, social media harassment, to identity theft. Cybercriminals exploit personal information, manipulate social interactions, and sometimes even attempt physical harm by tracking online activity.

Statistics show that a significant number of women experience harassment or threats online every year. Yet, awareness and tools for self-protection are still limited.

Why We Built SafeHer

SafeHer was created to empower women by offering practical tools and resources for online safety. Our goal is to:

  • Provide easy access to emergency contacts and helplines.
  • Offer guidance on recognizing and responding to cyber threats.
  • Educate about safe online practices through videos, tips, and tutorials.
  • Promote a supportive community for sharing experiences safely.

By combining awareness, real-time help, and educational resources, SafeHer aims to make the digital world safer for women everywhere.
